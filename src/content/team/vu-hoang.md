Vu is a PhD student supervised by Prof. Naomi Harte. His research focuses 
on multimodal speech understanding, particularly how multimodal signals 
facilitate agile turn-taking in both human–human and human–robot conversations. 
His long-term goal is to contribute to the development of full-duplex speech 
systems capable of more natural interactive communication.
Before joining Trinity College Dublin, Vu worked on a range of speech recognition
and speech classification problems in both research and industry. He was an 
undergraduate research student at Hanoi University of Science and Technology, 
Vietnam, and later a research intern at the Agency for Science, Technology
and Research (A*STAR), Singapore.
